ABOUT US

Welcome to HJ store, where passion meets creativity on the canvas of your everyday wear! We’re a print-on-demand t-shirt store that believes in turning your ideas into wearable art. Whether you’re all about bold statements, unique designs, or just something fun and quirky, we’ve got you covered—literally.

At HJ store, every t-shirt tells a story. Our mission is simple: to provide high-quality, custom-printed tees that reflect your individuality. We work with talented artists and designers from around the world to bring you a diverse range of styles, from minimalist chic to loud and proud.

Our process is as unique as you are. We only print what you love—no mass production, no waste. This means every t-shirt you buy is freshly made just for you, with eco-friendly inks and materials that are as comfortable as they are durable. Plus, our easy-to-use platform lets you personalize your design or choose from our ever-growing collection.

We’re more than just a store; we’re a community of creators, dreamers, and trendsetters. So, whether you’re here to find your new favorite tee or to create one of your own, we’re excited to be part of your style journey.

Thanks for choosing HJ Store. Wear your passion. Share your story.

FAQs

1. How long does it take to receive my order?

We typically process and print your order within 2-5 business days. Shipping times vary depending on your location, but most orders arrive within 7-14 business days. You'll receive a tracking number as soon as your order ships, so you can keep an eye on its journey to you!

2. Can I customize a design or create my own?

Absolutely! Our platform makes it easy to personalize existing designs or create your own from scratch. Just choose your t-shirt style, upload your design or text, and preview it before ordering. If you need help, our design team is here to assist.

3. What is your return or exchange policy?

Since each t-shirt is custom-made to order, we only accept returns or exchanges for items that are defective or damaged. If you encounter any issues, please contact our customer support team within 30 days of receiving your order, and we’ll make it right.
